Intro

My name is Dorisgloor Abaiweh. I currently attend The New School University in New York City. In this blog, I hope to share with you all my interests and hobbies. A little about myself: I was born in a small town called "Buea" in Cameroon. I came to the States with my family when I was about nine years old. When we came to the States, we resided in Northern Virginia. Because we lived so close to Washington DC, I grew accustomed to living in the city and came to the realization that I could only attend a school that was in a city. I refused to attend a school in Virginia or DC because I desperately needed a chage. When I visited The New School my senior year of high school, I knew it was the school for me. And two years later, her I am.
